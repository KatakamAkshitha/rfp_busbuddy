CVR COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

Welcome to Bus Buddy

Your reliable mode of transportation on campus.

Our History

Since 2001, CVR COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING Bus Transportation Service has been an integral part of campus life, ensuring that students can easily navigate the campus and surrounding areas. Over the years, we have continuously improved our services to better serve the needs of our community.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of transportation services designed to make getting around campus easy and hassle-free. Our services include:

  • Regular Campus Shuttle Routes
  • Off-Campus Housing Shuttles
  • Special Event Shuttles
  • Weekend Excursions
  • Accessibility Services for Students with Disabilities

Safety and Sustainability

The safety and well-being of our passengers are our top priorities. Our buses are regularly inspected and maintained to the highest safety standards, and our drivers undergo rigorous training to ensure safe and responsible operation. Additionally, we are committed to reducing our environmental impact by incorporating eco-friendly practices into our operations.

Convenience and Accessibility

We strive to make transportation as convenient and accessible as possible for everyone in the CVR COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING community. Our bus routes are carefully planned to provide coverage to all areas of campus and nearby off-campus locations. We also offer real-time tracking and scheduling information through our mobile app, making it easy to plan your journey and minimize wait times.

Customer Satisfaction

We value the feedback of our passengers and are constantly seeking ways to improve our services. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are here to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we're dedicated to providing the best possible transportation experience for everyone at CVR COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING.

Join Us

Whether you're heading to class, meeting friends, or exploring the area, CVR COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING Bus Transportation Service is here to help you get where you need to go. Sit back, relax, and enjoy the ride!

Thank you for choosing Bus Buddy. We look forward to serving you!
